About

I just finished my master at Karadeniz Technical University. In my thesis, I studied effect of general neutirno interactions within coherent elastic neutrino-nucleus scattering under supervision of Mehmet Demirici. The phenomena provides the highest measurable quantity of process involving neutrino and can be observed in relatively low energy. The process can open new paradigms of understanding neutrino interactions with matter in low energy region.
